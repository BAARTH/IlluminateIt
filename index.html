How to play_

"Commit to the glorious One, as He's unique and omniscient"

In this slow paced clicker game, all you have to do is to lead humanity to enlightment by feeding the sun, and harvest the lumens it gives you back.

Elements of gameplay

Performance_

It's the productivity of the Sun. Click on it to maintain it to peak levels as it'll decrease progressively.

Lumens_

It's the quantity of light energy stored inside the sun. Click on "Unleash the light" below to harvest it !

Illuminers_

Those are your great light devotees. They help you to feed the star, each one of them produces 1 Lumen per second

Illumination_

This is the faith Sun's adepts have. It helps you to build more buildings to get more Illuminers, or to buy upgrades. You gain 1 for each 10 Lumens you harvest.

Buildings_

Those are the main source of illuminers and, by extension, Lumens. Build more with illumination to produce lumens faster. Each one of them enlarge your star's lumen capacity.

Mooners and Eclipses_

Mooners are your ennemies, as they pray a false god, the moon. The more illuminers you have, the more likely they're to appear : a lot of mooners will make eclipses happen more often (and you'll have to break the moon in order to continue gathering lumens).

Enjoy the game and don't forget to Praise the sun !
